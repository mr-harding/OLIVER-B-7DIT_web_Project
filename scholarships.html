SCHOLARSHIPS

One scholarship we offer is a full ride scholarship. Here the athlete will have his/her entire education andliving costs funded by the school itself with no exceptions. We like to offer this to students in need who may not have the same oppurtunities as their peers.

Another scholarship we offer is a part ride scholarship, where the athlete will have SOME of his/her living expenses paid for along with a free education, this is for students who may be not considering university due to high costs but who have potential and could be an asset to our programme.

Our other scholarship we offer is our education scholarship. Where the student has to pay for all their living expenses and programme fees yet recieves a free education. This is a scholarship we hand out heavily as it is not very resource heavy. It gives the student an oppurtunity to play college sport without worrying too much about the expenses.
