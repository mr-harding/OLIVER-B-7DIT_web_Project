SPORTS

Our rugby programme here at Biz state is very strong. We have over 500 players, 27 teams, 32 dedicated coaches and 2 strength trainers. We also have 8 full fields, a full gymnasium and a plethora of equipment. We have had a lot of success recently including winning the varsity final for the southwest division. We have had 40 national representative players on our teams.

Here at biz state we pride ourselves in our rowing programme. We have a fleet of high quality boats built by shellmakers such as enpacher, fillipi and hudson. We have 10 dedicated coaches and a 10km stretch of water for our oarsmen to hone their craft. Our recent successes include winning 2019 varsity eight in the atlantic division. We have had 10 olympic rowers come from our university

Our swimming program at Biz state is very strong. We have a full training facility including 3 olympic sized pools, an altitude simulation training room and a full gymnasium decidated to the swimmers. We had 15 top 3 finishes over the 32 events we contested at the recent national swimming champs. 23 olympic swimmers have been through our university.
